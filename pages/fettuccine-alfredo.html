Creamy Mushroom Spaghetti

This creamy mushroom spaghetti blends tender mushrooms with rich cream and garlic for a satisfying vegetarian pasta dish.

Ingredients

  • 350g spaghetti
  • 300g mushrooms, sliced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • Fresh parsley, chopped (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions; drain and set aside.
  2. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté garlic until fragrant.
  3. Add mushrooms and cook until soft and browned.
  4. Pour in heavy cream and simmer for 3-4 minutes.
  5. Stir in Parmesan cheese until melted and sauce thickens.
  6. Combine sauce with spaghetti; season with salt and pepper.
  7. Garnish with fresh parsley and serve hot.
