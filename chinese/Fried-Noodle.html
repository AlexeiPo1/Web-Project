Singapore Fried Rice Noodles

Singapore fried rice noodles are a colorful stir-fry dish made with thin rice vermicelli, curry powder, vegetables, and your choice of meat or prawns. It’s fragrant, slightly spicy, and full of flavor.

Ingredients

  • 200g rice vermicelli noodles
  • 2 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 onion, sliced
  • 1 red bell pepper, sliced
  • 1 carrot, julienned
  • 100g cooked prawns or chicken strips
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp curry powder
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • Spring onions for garnish
<

Instructions

  1. Soak rice noodles in hot water for 5–7 minutes, then drain.
  2. Heat oil in a wok. Scramble eggs, then remove and set aside.
  3. Add garlic, onion, and vegetables, stir-fry 3–4 minutes.
  4. Add prawns or chicken, then noodles.
  5. Stir in soy sauce, curry powder, and sesame oil. Toss well.
  6. Add scrambled eggs back in, garnish with spring onions, and serve hot.
